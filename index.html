ABOUT ME

I love problem solving

I started my time at Vista in a Pricing Specialist role and quickly found that I love building tools and working with data. The natural step from here was to teach myself some basic programming and before long I was in a new role as an analyst in the cross-functional pricing & promo data product team. Here my programming skills developed faster and I started developing scalable solutions to tracking the impact from price changes - helping me to develop skills across data analytics, science & enginnering.
A major breakthrough occured when I was able to innovate a new approach to tracking price change impact through time-split testing. This required working in an area entirely new for the company allowing us to measure price changes far more reliably bringing greater value for the business and customers. Leading the impact tracking workstream put myself in a great position to develop the experimentation methodology from scratch and build an entirely new automated and scalable data product end-to-end.
